A HEALTHY PLANET

GOAL 13- CLIMATE ACTION

Hold on tight because climate change is a big deal, touching every corner of our planet. We're not fully ready for the challenges it brings.

Humans are behind this, and it's seriously threatening life on Earth. The way we're pumping out greenhouse gases is making climate change happen way faster than expected. Brace yourself for extreme weather and rising seas. If we don't act now, the progress we've made over the years will crumble. Massive migrations, instability, and wars could become our reality. To keep global warming in check, we need to slash emissions by almost half by 2030. Right now, we're way off course. It's time for serious action and more than just plans and promises. We need big ideas that cover whole economies. Let's move towards development that can handle climate challenges, with a clear path to zero emissions. Quick measures are a must to dodge a disaster and create a lasting future for all. Join the movement!

Why is this all important?

Our planet is facing a serious problem called climate change. It's happening because of things we do, like driving cars and using factories that release heat into the air. This makes the Earth too warm, causing problems like floods and storms. We will because of this feel the effects, no matter where we live, If we don't do something about it, things will get even worse. We're already seeing changes in weather, and it's affecting our health, food, and homes.

We need to work harder

Climate change is a big problem for our Earth. It happens because people use things like cars and factories that make the Earth too warm. This warming causes many issues like floods and storms. If we don't act, the cost will be much higher. So, we need to work hard to keep our promise and invest in a safer, better future for everyone.

6 easy stepts to take climate action:

  1. Turn off lights when not needed.
  2. Sort your trash for recycling.
  3. Use both sides of paper.
  4. Pick up trash when you see it.
  5. Use your legs instead of a car or bike more!
  6. Plant a small flower in your backyard.
